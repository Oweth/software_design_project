Sell on Artify


Looking to become a seller? Here's how to get started.

  1. Login to your profile, or create on if you don't already have one.
  2. Click on the link "Do you want to sell your homemade work?" the top-left corner of the Home page.
  3. Fill in the registration form.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Home page, and under the "Shop" heading in the footer, find the link "View My Shop".
  5. Upon clicking on the link, you will land on your dashboard as the seller.
  6. On the left hand side of the page, you will see the option to "Add Products". This is to add products in your shop.
  7. Cliking on "Add Products" will lead you to a form that you will need to fill out in order to add your products.
  8. After adding your products, you should receive an alert of your successful addition of your products.
  9. Your products will be available to find on the Home screen - you can search for them by name, or by category.
